I've had the great opportunity to work with folks over at the Way for Life NGO on a project to improve the quality of education for the underprivileged. Identifying the problem of high dropout rates in government schools due to poor subject material, we decided to put together multi-lingual educational podcasts that approached the subjects of Science and Social Studies in a more engaging and activity-based manner. It was also necessary to create a medium of access for these children. Hence, through a partnership with Philips, we are looking to provide them with MP3 players, which can hold the necessary subject material in one place. With a team of over 20 invested individuals, this is an active project with the intent and potential of bringing positive change.
