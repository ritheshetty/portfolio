I enjoy conceptualizing and building things from scratch. As a pastime, working with different materials to create the smallest of things excites me. "Build over buy" is a philosophy I came across recently, and in our uber-consumptive world today, I think there's tremendous value in it with respect to sustainability and skill development.

Here are a few things I've created:

  • Jenga phone holder

    • A sturdy phone holder made out of Jenga blocks, with a provision for the charging wire.


  • PVC-based height-adjustable laptop stand

    • An innovative laptop stand made out of common PVC pipes interconnected to create a steady structure. Also, it is height-adjustable and uses stoppers to avoid sliding.


  • Automated table-top catapult

    • A tabletop catapult with fully automated reload capacity using Arduino-controlled motors
